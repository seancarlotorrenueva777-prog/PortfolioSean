About Me

I am a dedicated multi-instrumentalist who began my musical journey at the age of 7, starting with piano for four years. Over time, I expanded into bass and lead guitar, performing in bands and participating in street acoustic sessions.

Currently, I serve as a lead guitarist in church while continuing to produce and write original music. Coming from a family of musicians, I developed my craft through self-learning, discipline, and passion.

I have a strong interest in acoustic looping and aim to inspire beginner guitarists to grow, create, and express themselves through music.
